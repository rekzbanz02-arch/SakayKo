Help & Guide
Everything you need to know about SakayKo
How do I request a ride? ▼
Go to the New Request tab, fill in your starting location and destination, then tap Submit Request. Your request will be sent to the driver instantly. After submitting, you'll be taken to My Requests automatically.
How do I track my request? ▼
Check the My Requests tab. It loads instantly — no waiting needed! Color-coded statuses: 🟡 Pending → 🔵 Accepted → 🟣 In Progress → 🟢 Completed. The list refreshes automatically every 8 seconds.
How do I see where my driver is? ▼
Two ways: (1) The teal bar at the top of the screen shows the driver's online status and has a 📍 Driver Location button — tap it anytime to see the driver on the map. (2) When your request is Accepted or In Progress, a 🗺 Driver Location button also appears on your request card.
How do I message the driver? ▼
Each request card has a 💬 Message button. If the button is blinking orange, it means you have a new unread message from the driver! Tap it to open the chat. Messages are saved and synced in real time.
Why should I enable location? ▼
Your GPS coordinates are saved with your request so the driver knows exactly where to pick you up. You'll be prompted automatically when you open the app — just tap Allow when your browser asks. The banner at the top of the screen shows your location status at all times.
What do the driver status colors mean? ▼
The teal bar at the top shows the driver's location status: 🟢 Teal means the driver's location was updated recently (within 10 minutes). 🟡 Amber means the last update was a while ago. If the bar is not visible, the driver may be offline or location sharing is turned off.
Is my name stored? ▼
Yes — your name is saved locally on your device so you don't have to type it every time. It is never shared beyond the ride request system.

Driver Guide
How to manage requests and the system
How do I accept a passenger request? ▼
Tap ✅ Accept on any pending request card. This marks the request as Accepted and notifies the passenger. Navigation does not open yet — it opens when you start the trip.
How do I start navigation to the passenger? ▼
After accepting, tap 🛣️ Start Trip + Navigate. This marks the trip as In Progress and immediately opens Google Maps with the full route: your current location → passenger pickup → destination. Navigation only opens at this step so you're ready to move.
How do I see where the passenger is? ▼
Each request card has a 📍 Client Map button. If the passenger shared GPS, this opens navigation directly to their exact coordinates. Otherwise it uses their text description as the destination.
How does location sharing work? ▼
When you log in as driver, your GPS is requested automatically. Your coordinates are saved to the cloud every 30 seconds so passengers can tap 📍 Driver Location on their screen to see where you are on the map. Make sure location permission is granted in your browser.
How do I message a passenger? ▼
Tap the 💬 Message button on any request card. If the button is blinking orange, it means the passenger sent you a new message! Tap it to open the chat. Use the quick-reply buttons for common responses like "Coming na!" or "5 mins pa."
What does the blinking orange Message button mean? ▼
A blinking orange 💬 Message 🔴 button means the passenger sent you a new unread message. Tap it to open the chat — the button will stop blinking once you've read the messages.
What is the default driver location? ▼
In Settings, you can set a fallback latitude/longitude. If real-time GPS is unavailable (e.g. permission denied or no signal), this default is used instead for navigation links shown to passengers.
What sheets does this app use? ▼
The app uses four sheets: Requests (trip data), Messages (chat per request), Config (driver location, settings), and Password (admin password). All are managed automatically.
